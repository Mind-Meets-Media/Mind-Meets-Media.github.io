1st Edition (2024)

The first edition of the SMS workshop was held in 2024, in Rende (CS), Italy as an ASONAM satellite event. The workshop aimed to bring together researchers and practitioners from academia to discuss the latest advances in the field of social media and human behavior. The workshop featured a mix of paper presentations, abstracts, and invited talks.
